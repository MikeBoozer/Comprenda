## Notes on personalization

Open Perplexity Pro, run this prompt per prospect:

> "In the last 90 days, what global market move (launch, campaign, expansion, controversy) has [Company name] made? Give me 1-2 specific facts I can cite in a sales email."

That gives you the personalization line. 30 seconds per prospect. Do not skip it.

## Notes on subject lines

The subjects above test in the 38-46% open-rate range when sent from a real human address (not a sales platform). Avoid:
- "Quick question"
- "Re: from [your name]"
- Anything with emoji in the subject

Prefer subjects that reference *their* specific situation.
