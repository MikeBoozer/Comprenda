What Nuance does

⚠️ Pre-Launch Cultural Risk Score

Paste a draft tagline. Pick target markets. Get a 0-100 cultural risk score per market in 8 seconds — grounded in semantically-similar historical content and our cultural frame taxonomy.

🌍 Cultural Translator

Beyond translation. Generate 2-3 culturally-adapted variants that preserve intent but shift the cultural frame for the target market. Drop-in usable in your workflow.

🔥 Cultural Divergence Score

Proprietary metric — the only one of its kind. Quantifies how differently language communities reacted to the same event at the semantic level, not just sentiment polarity.

🔔 Cultural Drift Alerts

Subscribe a brand or campaign. We monitor cross-lingual divergence hourly. When it spikes, you get an email or Slack notification before it becomes a crisis.

🧭 Analog Retrieval

Pattern matching as competitive moat. Your current campaign profile gets matched against 100+ documented historical cases (HSBC, Mercedes, Pepsi, Dolce & Gabbana). See what happened then.

Brand-tier customers expose Nuance as a cultural-context tool any Claude, Cursor, or internal agent can call. Cultural QA becomes a single function call inside every workflow.
