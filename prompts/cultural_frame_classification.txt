## Frame definitions (for reference)

- individualist: personal autonomy, individual rights, self-expression
- collectivist: group harmony, family/community obligations, shared identity
- nationalist: national pride, in-group preference, cultural sovereignty
- globalist: cross-cultural exchange, international cooperation, cosmopolitanism
- threat_framing: danger, loss, vulnerability
- opportunity_framing: benefit, growth, possibility
- historical_grievance: past wrongs, colonial memory, ethnic or political grievance
- status_quo: preservation, tradition, established norms
- reform_seeking: change, progress, structural improvement
- spiritual_ethical: moral, religious, or ethical framing
- pragmatic: practical outcomes, efficiency, results
- ambiguous: no clear frame; mixed or unclear signaling
